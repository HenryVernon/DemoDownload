The 1975

About

The 1975 are a British pop rock band from Wilmslow, Cheshire, Now based in Manchester. They are known for their sound ranging from slow ballads to upbeat hoppy pop tunes. The band consists of lead singer Matthew Healy, lead guitarist Adam Hann, bassist Ross Macdonald, and drummer/producer George Daniel.
